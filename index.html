Instructions



  1. In this game there are two players: Player1 and Player2.
  2. You need to start the game by clicking on Roll Dice button and your dice number will become your current score. How many times you play, your dice number will keep get add in the current score.
  3. If your dice number comes as 1 then you loose the game and other player will get active.
  4. If you press Hold button your current score will get add to total score and you will loose your trun and other player will get active.
  5. If any player score 20, then you are the winner.
  6. You can reset game at any point of time by clicking on New Game button.
